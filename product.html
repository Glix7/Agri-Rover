ABOUT THE PRODUCT

One Robot. Many Jobs.

AgriRover is a versatile robotic platform that brings the power of industrial-grade automation to India's small farmers. With swappable tool attachments, it handles spraying, sowing, and fertilizing — reducing labor costs and increasing yield.

✔ Steel frame built for Indian field conditions
✔ 10-inch rugged wheels for uneven terrain
✔ Up to 1km wireless remote range
✔ Interchangeable tool attachment system
✔ Low-power, long-duration operation
✔ Weather-resistant enclosed electronics
