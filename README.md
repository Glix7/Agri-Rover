🚜 AgriRover – AI-Powered Smart Farming Revolution

AgriRover is an intelligent autonomous agricultural rover designed to empower small and marginal farmers with affordable, scalable, and AI-driven farming solutions.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between advanced agricultural technology and real farming needs by making smart farming accessible to everyone.

🌱 Vision

To revolutionize Indian agriculture through affordable AI robotics that increase productivity, reduce labor dependency, and improve crop efficiency.

🚀 Features
Autonomous field navigation
AI-powered crop monitoring
Soil health analysis integration
Smart irrigation assistance
Modular farming attachments
Real-time IoT sensor connectivity
Scalable for small and medium farms

💡 Problem We Solve

86% of Indian farmers are small-scale farmers, yet most agricultural machinery is designed for large industrial farms.
AgriRover solves this by:
Lowering technology cost barriers
Providing compact smart machinery
Enabling precision agriculture for all farmers
