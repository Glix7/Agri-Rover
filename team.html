What the engineering team covers

Capability areas we are building (and hiring into) as AgriRover matures.

Mechanical & hardware

Chassis, actuators, power and sensors — designed for mud, dust, and long days in the sun.

Embedded & firmware

Microcontrollers, motor control, wireless links, and safe, deterministic behavior on the robot.

Software & AI

Field software, data from trials, and models that make spraying and navigation smarter over time.

Advisors

Domain experts who help us stress-test assumptions and stay grounded in real agriculture.

Agriculture advisor

Technology mentor

Startup support
